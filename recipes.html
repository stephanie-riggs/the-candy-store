PICK N MIX ICE LOLLIES

Ingredients

  • 160g Haribo Tangfastics
  • 100g Gummy Bears
  • 75g Fizzy Rainbow Belts
  • 680ml Lemonade

Method

  1. Fill ice lolly moulds with a selection of sweets.
  2. Fill the moulds to the top with lemonade.
  3. Push in a wooden stick.
  4. Place the moulds in the freezer for a minimum of 5 hours.

COTTON CANDY FUDGE

Ingredients

  • 400g Sugar
  • 170g Butter
  • 340g White Chocolate
  • 200g Marshmallow Fluff
  • 180g Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2tsp Cotton Candy Flavour
  • 2tsp Pink Food Colouring

Method

  1. Line a 13x9 inch tin with foil and spray with a non-stick spray.
  2. Combine the sugar, butter and cream in a saucepan, and stir on a low heat until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Once the sugar has dissolved, bring the pan to boil for 4 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and add the marshmallow fluff and white chocolate, then stir until melted.
  5. Pour 3/4 of the liquid into the foil lined tin.
  6. Add cotton candy flavouring to the rest of the mixture in the pan, then pour into the tin.
  7. Add 3 drops of pink food colouring to various places in the tin, using a knife to swirl the colour.
  8. Cover the tin, and allow to set in the fridge.
  9. Once cooled, remove the fudge from the tin, slice into squares, and serve.

RICE KRISPIE TREATS

Ingredients

  • 45g Butter
  • 280g Marshmallows
  • 200g Rice Krispies

Method

  1. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over a low heat.
  2. Once the butter is melted, add the marshmallows and stir until completely melted.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat.
  4. Add the rice krispies and stir until completely coated.
  5. Scoop the mixture into a 13x9 inch tin coated in a non-stick spray.
  6. Place in the fridge to cool.
  7. Once cooled, cut into squares and serve.

HOMEMADE PEPPERMINT PATTIES

Ingredients

  • 60g Softened Butter
  • 115g Light Corn Syrup
  • 2tsp Peppermint Extract
  • 300g Powdered Sugar
  • 350g Dark Chocolate

Method

  1. Combine the butter, corn syrup, powdered sugar and peppermint extract into a mixing bowl.
  2. Stir the mixture with an electric whisk until everything is combined.
  3. Remove the dough from the bowl and roll into walnut sized balls.
  4. Place the dough balls onto a baking tray covered in parchment paper and flatten the gently.
  5. Place the tray in the freezer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Melt the dark chocolate, then let it sit for 1 minute.
  7. Remove the peppermint dough balls from the freezer and dip them into the melted chocolate, then place them back on the baking tray.
  8. Leave to dry, and keep in the fridge.
