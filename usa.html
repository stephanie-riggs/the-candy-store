Twizzlers

Twizzlers are an American staple, often referred to as a licorice-type candy, they in fact contain no liquorice at all, instead they are mainly made from corn syrup and fruit flavoured syrup. The iconic red twizzler is strawberry flavoured, but they now come in a large variety including blue raspberry, watermelon and cherry.

Jolly Ranchers

Jolly Ranchers are a household name in the States. Originating from Denver, Colorado in 1949 they were first sold in ice cream stores in the Denver area under 3 flavours, watermelon, apple and fire stix. They are now owned by the Hersheys company and come in a large quantity of flavours, and have also expanded from just the original hard boiled form.

Reese’s Peanut Butter Cups

Reese’s Peanut Butter Cups are now seen all over the world, but they were originally created by by a man named Harry Burnett (H.B.) Reese in 1879. They are now under the Hersheys company and come in a variety of products such as easter eggs, special edition flavours and snack mixes.
